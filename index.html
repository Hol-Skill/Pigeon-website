Facts about Pigeons

Here are some interesting facts about Pigeons

  • Pigeons are descended from domesticated birds, which means most pigeon breeds you see are actually feral, not wild.

  • Before chickens, pigeons were commonly bred for their meat.

  • Pigeons can have 3 broods of chicks a year.

  • Pigeons were one of our first couriers and aerial camera operators.

The most common breeds of pigeon we see in the UK are:

  1. Rock Dove
  2. Wood Pigeon
  3. Ring tailed Pigeon
